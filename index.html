Popular Times to Sell Property

Saturday remains the most popular day to sell property. While this is a blessing for sellers, this often means there is much more competition for buyers. Buyers often feel there are greater options on the market over the weekend, in particular on Saturdays, so they tend to hold off committing to a sale on a weekday.

The worst months to sell property would be the period between December and February as most potential buyers are wrapped up in holiday plans and travel or they are focusing on purchasing gifts as opposed to saving for a downpayment.

The drastic decrease in properties sold between August 2017 and November 2017 can be attributed to the surge in property prices.
